Breadsticks

Hang on. Asher is making breadsticks copied from Olive Garden? Hang on, folks. (Note: Asher has never been to Olive Garden nor does he intend to go.)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup + 2T warm water (110 - 115 degrees)
  • 1.25t active dry yeast
  • 2T granulated sugar
    • divided
  • 3 - 3.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1.75t salt
  • 3T vegetable oil or olive oil

Topping:

  • 2T butter
  • 1/4t salt
  • 1/4t garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Make bread using the ingredients listed. No surprises here.
  2. Rise for 1.5 hours.
  3. Make into 12 bread sticks. Put them on parchment paper. Brush them with oil. Cover with saran wrap (the oil keeps this from sticking). Rise for an hour.
  4. Bake at 425F for 11 - 13 minutes until golden.
  5. Brush with the delicious topping. Probably double it. Butter is good.
  6. Eat.

Chef’s Notes

It’s just bread. I’m not sure what differentiates this from other bread recipes. The topping is really good, and these were delicious. I’m sure they were better than Olive Garden, even though I have never been there.

Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups bread flour, plus more for rolling
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 envelope instant dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water, 110 degrees F
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus 2 teaspoons

Instructions

  1. Combine the bread flour, sugar, yeast and kosher salt in the bowl of a stand mixer and combine. While the mixer is running, add the water and 2 tablespoons of the oil and beat until the dough forms into a ball. If the dough is sticky, add additional flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, until the dough comes together in a solid ball. If the dough is too dry, add additional water, 1 tablespoon at a time. Scrape the dough onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead into a smooth, firm ball.
  2. Grease a large bowl with the remaining 2 teaspoons olive oil, add the dough, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and put it in a warm area to let it double in size, about 1 hour. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and divide it into 2 equal pieces. Cover each with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let them rest for 10 minutes.

Notes

  • Knead with hands. Work around the volume of water, adding flour as needed.
  • Give the dough time to rise!

Tortillas

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4.5 Tablespoons oil
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and water until pretty hot, approx. 140 F.
  2. Mix with flour and salt and knead until combined.

Notes

  • A bit of bread flour (1 out of 3 cups) makes the tortillas sturdier.
